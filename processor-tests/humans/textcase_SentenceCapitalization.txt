Used to be: "This is a pen that is a Smith pencil", but this was incorrect:

> ##### Sentence Case Conversion
> ...
>
> 2. For lower or mixed case strings, the first character of the first word is
> capitalized if the word is lowercase. **The case of all other words stays the
> same.**

Hence, Pen and Pencil should remain capitalized.

>>===== RESULT =====>>
This is a Pen that is a Smith Pencil
<<===== RESULT =====<<
